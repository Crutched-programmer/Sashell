What is SaShell?

SaShell is a Python-based Linux shell that runs real commands natively вҖ” pipes, redirects, background jobs, everything bash supports.

The AI doesn't translate your words into commands. It watches what you type and only reacts when something is dangerous, misspelled, or just a meme.

It also packs a full conversational AI chat mode, Sarvam-powered text-to-speech in 16 Indian voices, multi-language support, and a terminal personality that refuses to be boring.
